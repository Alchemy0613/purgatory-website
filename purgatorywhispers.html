Welcome to the Whispering Grounds

Where the forgotten speak, and the mountain listens.

Beneath the neon glow and Appalachian mist, stories cling to the dark. Some are ancient. Some are restless. Some are waiting for the right voices to bring them back into the light.

What Is Purgatory Whispers?

Purgatory Whispers is a paranormal storytelling podcast rooted in the haunted ridges, forgotten towns, and myth-soaked hollows of Appalachia. Each episode blends folklore, history, and first-hand accounts with a neon‑charged, eerie‑playful energy that’s become our signature.

We don’t just tell ghost stories — we follow the threads, chase the whispers, and dig into the places where truth and legend blur.

Why We Whisper

Some stories don’t want to be shouted. Some truths only reveal themselves in quiet places. And some voices — the lost, the restless, the forgotten — can only be heard when you lean in close.

We whisper because the mountain remembers. And it remembers everything.
