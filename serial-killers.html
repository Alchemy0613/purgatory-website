The Anatomy of a Predator

Serial killers aren’t born from darkness — they’re shaped by it. Childhood trauma, psychological fractures, environmental triggers, and opportunity all collide to create the kind of predator that hunts in patterns.

This page explores some of the most infamous killers in history, their methods, their psychology, and the signatures that made them unforgettable.
