The Ones Who Never Came Home

Cold cases are more than unsolved crimes — they’re unfinished stories. Missing persons are more than statistics — they’re people who vanished without explanation, leaving families suspended in time.

This page is dedicated to the faces that deserve answers, the names that deserve to be spoken, and the cases that still whisper through the cracks of small‑town America.

Featured Case

Michelle Jolene Lakey — Missing Since 1986

Name: Michelle Jolene Lakey

Missing From: Scranton, Pennsylvania

Year: 1986

Status: Missing — Cold Case

Michelle was last seen walking near her home in Scranton. She was young, bright, and full of life — and then she was gone. No confirmed sightings. No arrests. No closure.

Her case remains one of the most haunting unsolved disappearances in Northeast Pennsylvania.
